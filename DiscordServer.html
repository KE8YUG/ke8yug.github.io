Become a member of #TeamHam and join us in TheYUG Discord server!

The idea here is to create an online community that can help us all stay together on various clubs, their events, and share our passion for radio.  If we can build and foster a community in Discord, we can potentially reach a whole different audience and generate more interest in HAM radio.  Please join us in making this a reality, and have a lot of fun doing it! You do not need to have an amateur radio license to join this server. If you are interested in the hobby, come say hello and learn more!

Note: After you join TheYUG Discord server, we will need to assign you a Role to be able to fully interact with everyone on the server.  Once we receive the notification that you have joined the server, we will act as quickly as possible to get you assigned to the #TeamHam Role.  Please be patient as we are still in the process of implementing bots to handle this task.
