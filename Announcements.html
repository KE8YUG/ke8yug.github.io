March 6th, 2024

Hello! Thank you for stopping by and I am glad you are here!

I created this website as a front-end for myself, as well as a place to launch some projects and passions of mine.  I plan to use this space to showcase some of my favorite parts of the radio hobby while using it as a vehicle to get out updates of what I might be working on.
